Pondicherry University Complaint Management System

Powered by University Administration

Pondicherry University Complaint Management System is a comprehensive solution that empowers students, faculty, and staff to report issues, identify maintenance needs, share feedback, and ask questions of university administration. For university officials, it powers efficient and transparent workflows, fostering accountability and trust. The result is collaborative experiences between the university community and administration that co-create clean, safe, and well-maintained campus environments.

How It Works

Simple steps to report and resolve campus issues

Report an Issue

Easily report maintenance issues, facility problems, or other concerns using our simple form.

Track Progress

Monitor the status of your reported issues in real-time with our tracking system.

Get Resolved

Receive notifications when your issues are resolved and provide feedback on the process.
